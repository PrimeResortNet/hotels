Our Handpicked Hotels

🎲 For the High Rollers: London’s Best Casino Hotels

  1. The Royal Ace Casino & Hotel
    Mayfair, London
    Tucked between Michelin-starred restaurants and high-end boutiques, The Royal Ace is where luxury meets adrenaline. The casino floor buzzes with energy day and night, offering everything from high-stakes poker to sleek slot machines. After a winning streak (or even if luck wasn’t on your side), retreat to their marble-clad suites—some with private terraces overlooking Hyde Park. Pro tip: Their espresso martinis at the in-house bar are legendary.
  2. The Vortex Grand
    Soho, London
    This place doesn’t just have a casino—it is an experience. The Vortex Grand feels like stepping into a James Bond film, with its moody lighting, rooftop blackjack tables, and a crowd that’s equal parts stylish and unpredictable. The rooms? Think velvet headboards, rainfall showers, and skyline views that make waking up worth it. If you’re not into gambling, their live jazz nights are a stellar backup plan.

🛏️ For the Relaxation Seekers: Classic London Elegance

  1. The Westminster Grace
    Westminster, London
    A stone’s throw from Big Ben and the Thames, this hotel is perfect for travelers who want history without the stuffiness. The rooms blend antique touches (think wrought-iron bed frames) with modern comforts (hello, heated bathroom floors). Breakfast here is a proper affair—order the full English and thank us later.
  2. The Garden Manor
    Kensington, London
    If you’ve ever wanted to feel like royalty, this is your spot. Set just behind Kensington Palace, The Garden Manor feels like a private countryside estate—except you’re in the middle of London. Their spa is a hidden gem (try the lavender-infused massage), and afternoon tea in the glass conservatory is a must.
